The Legacy of Hercules:

Accomplishment Description
Slay the Nemean Lion Heracles defeated a lion that was attacking the city of Nemea with his bare hands. After he succeeded he wore the skin as a cloak to demonstrate his power over the opponent he had defeated.
Slay the Lernaean Hydra Heracles, with the help of his nephew Iolaus, defeated the nine-headed Hydra, a serpent that grew two heads for each one severed. He then dipped his arrows in its poisonous blood, making them deadly.
Capture the Golden Hind of Artemis Heracles chased the sacred hind for a year before capturing it without harm. When confronted by Artemis, he explained his task and was allowed to bring it back alive to King Eurystheus.
Capture the Erymanthian Boar Heracles captured the massive, destructive boar and brought it back to Mycenae. His patience and perseverance helped him complete this challenging task.
Clean the Augean Stables in a Day Tasked with cleaning stables housing thousands of cattle, Heracles diverted two rivers to wash away the filth in a single day, showcasing his ingenuity and strength.
Slay the Stymphalian Birds Using a rattle given by Athena, Heracles scared the deadly, man-eating birds into flight and shot them down with his arrows, freeing the forest from their terror.
Capture the Cretan Bull Heracles captured the ferocious Cretan Bull, father of the Minotaur, which was wreaking havoc around Knossos. He carried it to Eurystheus, who later released it, and the bull terrorized Marathon until Theseus killed it.
Steal the Mares of Diomedes Heracles captured Diomedes' flesh-eating horses by feeding their master to them. After binding their mouths, he safely delivered the horses to Eurystheus, completing this gruesome task.
